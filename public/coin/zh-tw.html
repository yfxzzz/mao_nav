Game Instructions

How to Play

1. Click the "開始拋擲" button to begin the game.
2. Watch the coin flip animation.
3. View the result in the popup and on the page.
4. Check statistics and history records.

Usage Tips

• A result popup will appear after each toss, click "OK" to close.
• History keeps up to 20 records, oldest ones are automatically deleted.
• Statistics can be filtered by "All" or "Recent 10".
• Click "重置" to clear all history and statistics.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Space Trigger toss
R 重置遊戲
H Show/hide history
S Toggle stats range
