Game Rules

Minesweeper is a classic single-player puzzle game. The objective is to locate all the mines hidden in the grid without detonating any of them.

Basic Operations:

  • Left Click: Reveal the cell content. If it's a mine, the game ends; if it's a number, it indicates the number of mines in the surrounding 8 cells; if it's blank, it automatically reveals surrounding safe areas.
  • Right Click: Mark suspicious mine locations. Each cell can have up to three states: unrevealed, flagged (red flag), and question mark.
  • Double Click on Revealed Number: When the number of flagged mines around a revealed number matches the number itself, double-clicking reveals all unmarked surrounding cells.

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: 9×9 grid, 10 mines
  • Medium: 16×16 grid, 40 mines
  • Hard: 16×30 grid, 99 mines

Winning Condition:

Successfully mark all mine locations or reveal all non-mine cells.
